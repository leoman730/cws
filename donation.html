DONATE TO CWS

The Children’s Workshop needs a little love, understanding and help!

CWS is striving to maintain our arts, music, dance, library, computer classes, class trips, and every other program we value, in the face of continuous and brutal budget reductions. Over the last five years, the DOE has reduced our school budget by nearly $500,000 resulting in an ever increasing burden on children and parents. Our budget needs this year equal $626 per child just to maintain the current programs. This amounts to $75 per month for the nine months of the school year.


  

Every little bit helps our school so please donate... and your time is needed as much as your donation. Donations can be mailed to the school, dropped off in the office or with your teacher, or made directly through PayPal.
