GAK Holiday Camp Schedule Outline

Ages 5-10 years old (K-5)

  • 8 am—Drop off | Breakfast

        Participants settle in. Breakfast will be provided.

  • 9-10am— Attendance | Morning Mindfulness

Participants gather and meet for mindful breathing and stretching to help get ready for the day. Youth practice sharing feelings and mindful listening.

  • 10-11am—Physical Activity | Sports | Team Building

        Youth are divided into two groups based on age (5-7 yrs old & 8-10 yrs old) to         maintain age appropriate activities. Physical activities will include: yoga, hula         hooping, basketball,         kickball, wiffle ball, and more.

  • 11am-12pm—Homework Help | Reading

Participants will be given the opportunity to complete homework assignments with support, and have reading time.

  • 12-1pm—Lunch | Free Time

Participants will be asked to bring in their own lunches. Weather permitting youth will spend time outdoors in a neighborhood playground/park.

  • 1-2pm –Mindfulness Activities

Activities will be taken from our GAK Mindfulness Curriculum and will include lessons on the brain, breathing techniques, 5 senses, emotions, and self-regulating behavior.

  • 2-3pm—Art | Craft Making

Each day will incorporate an art activity such as mural making, Lego building, collage, painting, clay, comic book making, and more.

  • 3-4pm—End of Day Community Meeting

Participants end the day with mindful breathing and stretching. Youth will be given another opportunity to share thoughts and feelings about their day and have a chance to discuss problems and solutions.

  • 4pm—Pickup

Parents sign their kids out.

* 4-6 possible extended day
